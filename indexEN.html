About us

Our main research interests are plant-pollinator interactions, floral ecology, pollen transfer and interactions with plant pathogens transferred by pollinators.

We study plants, pollinators and pathogens in Czech Republic, Central Europe. Our group is based at the Department of Botany, Faculty of Science Charles University.

At this page we would like to introduce our research topics , our team and news from our group.
